MPD: Why We Serve
Project Role:
Director, Cinematographer, & Video Editor.

Project Completion Date:
Oct. 13, 2022

Project Description:
MPD: Why We Serve (2022) is a 6 minute video production that provides viewers with a personal perspective into why Maui Police Department Officers serve Maui County. After seven months of shooting, interviewing, and editing, the final product was used in a Maui Police Department recruitment conference held in October (2022).
